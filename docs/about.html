About

Have you ever been stuck picking a lock and wished you could pull up an LPL video immediately to help?

That's all this site is; a simple search across his 1500+ videos without any funky algorithms, adverts or random videos from other people.

The LockPickingLawyer has no connection with this site, has probably not seen it, and certainly hasn't endorsed it.

This is a project by Robert Wallhead, created purely for fun and out of respect for LPL's contributions to locksport and security education.
